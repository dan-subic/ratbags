Fiction First

In Ratbags!, the fiction is all-important. Players can reason about the world around them, without number-crunching or referencing rules text.

Challenge Second

Ratbags! tries to present the players with challenging problems requiring creative solutions. Antics are encouraged.

Accessible to New Players

Ratbags! has lightning-fast character generation, and the rules can be easily learned at the table. Ideal for pick-up play and open table gaming.
